Monocycle is a fast paced arcade game where you have to balance not on a regular bike but on a monocycle. Once the game starts you can only influence if the monocycle shall lean forward or backward. If you lean too much to one side you will inevitable fall and you will break the monocycle. Now you might think it would be a good idea to drive very slowly with almost no slant but this is very, very wrong. You need every little bit of momentum to get past the hills. Otherwise you will come to a halt or even go back. Play Monocycle now for free and show your skills!
