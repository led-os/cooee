Ho Ho Ho! Santa Claus is coming to town in this very unique pinball style game. Santa bounces and jumps around and you have to pick up all the gifts that are scattered through the level. After picking up the presents Santa has to go back to the Merry Melody Note that acts as a gateway to the next level. Xmas Fever is easy to pick up for newcomers and provides challenges for seasoned veterans of the pinball genre. Simple controls and hilarious graphics make a fun and festive experience for everybody and all ages. You will quickly fall in love with Xmas Fever's charm and the challenges you will face!
